## Substantive empirical finding (capturing as drift-note 65)

**HT.0.9-verify's "Decision: (b)" framing collapsed two independent axes**. The 3-option block at parent plan line 160 read as mutually-exclusive alternatives:

> documentary personas could (a) join DEFAULT_ROSTERS, (b) keep `persona: <fixed>` shape, or (c) adopt `<set-at-spawn>` shape

But (a) and (b)/(c) are independent axes:
- (a) = DEFAULT_ROSTERS membership (substrate state — where personas are registered for cmdAssign roster lookup)
- (b) = contract `persona` field shape: fixed string ("14-codebase-locator")
- (c) = contract `persona` field shape: `<set-at-spawn>` placeholder (challenger-style)

(b) and (c) are alternatives for contract shape. (a) is orthogonal — independent decision about substrate state.

**HT.0.9-verify's "Decision: (b)" addressed contract shape only.** It did NOT address DEFAULT_ROSTERS membership. The current substrate state is: contract shape = (b) ✓ but DEFAULT_ROSTERS membership = absent. **This breaks `commands/research.md:62-67`'s invocation pattern at runtime.**
