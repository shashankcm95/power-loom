## Prioritization formula

**Impact (1-3 scale)**:
- 1 = Documentary / cosmetic / negligible function
- 2 = Substrate hygiene; 1+ phase carry-over; affects future sessions or substrate maintainability
- 3 = Active functional gap; blocks documented behavior; produces drift-source

**Effort (1-3 scale)**:
- 1 = Mechanical fix (single edit / small scope)
- 2 = Multi-file refactor (cross-cutting; 3-10 sites)
- 3 = Architectural decision required (multi-option; needs ADR or sub-plan)

**Score = Impact × Effort**. Higher score = more weight, with deprioritization for high-effort-low-impact items at the cap edge.
